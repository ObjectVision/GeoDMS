gdal.vect is the part of [[GDAL]], used to read [[vector data]] and non spatial data. 

We test and support (options for) the following formats:
- [[csv]]
- [[dbf]]  
- [[ESRI shapefile]] (point, arc, poly)
- [[geopackage]] 
- [[FileGeoDatabase]]
- GeoJSON 
- gml
- [[pbf (OSM)]]
