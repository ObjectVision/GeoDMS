Gdalwrite.vect is the part of [[GDAL]], used to write [[vector data]] and non spatial data. 

Gdalwrite.vect is available since version 7.408.

We test and support (options for) the following formats:
- [[csv]]
- [[dbf]]
- [[ESRI Shapefile]]
- [[geopackage]]
